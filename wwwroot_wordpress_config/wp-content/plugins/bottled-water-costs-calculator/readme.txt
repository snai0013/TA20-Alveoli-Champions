== Description ==

Find out your water cost by using our Bottled Water Calculator . 
This calculator can help you reducing your water costs and environmental impact .

== Installation ==

1. Upload the 'water-calc' folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Go to Widgets > WaterWidget and configure your calcultor
